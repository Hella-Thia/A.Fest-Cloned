About A-Fest


A-Fest is an invite-only community of 2000+ extraordinary people from 50+ countries who come together to connect, grow and learn from the world's best teachers. It takes place once a year in 5-star paradise locations across the world. These events gather an extraordinary community of change-makers and visionaries who are driven by epic ideas to impact the world – entrepreneurs, experts, artists, leaders, innovators, visionaries and more. A-Fest is like stepping into a different reality of wonder and magic. Since A-Fest is best expressed in visuals and not words, watch the After-Movie of previous events.
